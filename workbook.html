Practical tools for building your children's children

These worksheets are meant to be written in. Fill them in here and print your copy, or print them blank and fill them in by hand. The act of writing is what turns intention into structure. Revisit each on the schedule it suggests.

Shared Values Alignment

Purpose. To ensure all adult family members define wealth the same way. Each person answers individually, then the family compares answers and discusses the differences calmly. Tip: print one copy per adult.
